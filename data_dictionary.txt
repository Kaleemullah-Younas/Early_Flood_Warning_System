================================================================
DATASET 1: FloodSense_Training_Data.csv
================================================================
Source    : Provided by event organisers
Coverage  : Daily records, Dadu district, Sindh, Pakistan
Date range: January 2022 – December 2024

COLUMNS
-------
date               Date of the reading (DD/MM/YYYY)
district           Administrative district label
                   (Sindh_District, Balochistan_District, KP_District,
                   Nowshera, Jacobabad)
elevation          Station elevation in metres
evaporation        Daily evapotranspiration (mm). Negative values
                   indicate net moisture absorption.
latitude           Station latitude
longitude          Station longitude
precipitation      Daily rainfall (mm). Contains missing values (NaN)
                   for some district-date combinations.
pressure           Atmospheric pressure (Pa)
soil_moisture      Volumetric soil water content (0.0–1.0 scale)
temperature        Daily mean temperature (°C)
water_area_km2     Surface water extent (km²) derived from satellite
wind_speed         Daily mean wind speed (m/s)
humidity           Relative humidity (%)
precip_3day_avg    3-day rolling average of precipitation
precip_7day_avg    7-day rolling average of precipitation
temp_3day_avg      3-day rolling average of temperature
soil_3day_avg      3-day rolling average of soil moisture
day_of_year        Day number within the year (1–365)
month              Month number (1–12)
year               Calendar year (2022–2024)
is_monsoon         Binary flag: 1 = monsoon season, 0 = non-monsoon
water_area_change  Change in water_area_km2 from the previous day (km²)
water_area_pct_change  Percentage change in water area from previous day.
                       Contains very large values where prior
                       water_area_km2 was near or equal to zero.
ds_idx             Numeric index column
flood_event        TARGET: 0 = no flood, 1 = flood occurred


================================================================
DATASET 2: ndma_flood_impact_2022.csv
================================================================
Source   : National Disaster Management Authority (NDMA) Pakistan
           Provided by event organisers
Coverage : 2022 monsoon floods — regional aggregates across 7 regions
Purpose  : Regional-level flood impact data from the 2022 disaster.
           7 rows of regional totals.

COLUMNS
-------
region                  Administrative region (AJ&K, Balochistan, GB,
                        ICT, KP, Punjab, Sindh)
total_deaths            Total deaths
m_d                     Male deaths
f_d                     Female deaths
c_d                     Children deaths
total_injured           Total injured
m_i                     Male injured
f_i                     Female injured
c_i                     Children injured
roads_damaged_km_       Roads damaged (kilometres)
bridges_damaged         Number of bridges damaged
total_houses_damaged    Number of houses damaged
livestock_damaged       Number of livestock lost
affected_population     Total population affected

================================================================
DATASET 3: district_elevation_reference.csv
================================================================
Source   : NASA SRTM (Shuttle Radar Topography Mission)
Coverage : District-level average elevation for all 5 districts
           present in FloodSense_Training_Data.csv

COLUMNS
-------
district                District name
avg_elevation_m         Average elevation in metres above sea level
terrain_type            Terrain description
